As a Front-end Developer you will have created or contributed to an array of exciting projects. Tell us about the one your are most prouf of and why?
The project I am most proud of is working with Tennis Australia to reimagine and redesign the delivery of Tennis Victoria’s youth program, “Future Leaders”. Future Leaders facilitates youth to volunteer and support their local tennis clubs in camps. My team and I were assigned to digitalise and integrate the course component into Insosy’s Springboard Platform, thus allowing part of the program to be coordinated centrally and administered online. Our task was the central component in the program’s entire reimagining, and I thrived with this responsibility.
>
The project demanded the dedication of myself and my co-worker, working long hours to complete its implementation from planning to execution to analytics. As programmers, our understanding of the platform and the available technologies allowed us to aid Tennis Victoria to redefine their requirements and understand the potential of various programming options to illustrate and animate their content.
>
When it came to designing, we were in contact with a design agency which consolidated our knowledge of web page accessibility and usability, as well as industry best practices.We designed the web courses following Tennis Victoria's brand guidelines, colours, fonts, logos. At the end of the first camp, we prepared and shared analytics with Tennis Victoria.
>
For me personally, I took to heart some of the client’s feedback about how digitalizing the content had allowed the program to expand their community time and changed the Future Leaders Program to the core. I find much joy in web development when it facilitates ease for a group or company I am passionate about. Ultimately, I was also very proud of the design and interactivity of the webpages. My main takeaway from this project was to never limit the deliverables to my current skill set as a programmer. I learnt to listen to the client’s wants and needs and research which technology could be the solution to the challenge, then upskill myself.
